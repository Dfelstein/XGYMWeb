Who Is It For

Perfect for Everyone

The XGYM 2-week trial is designed for anyone curious about what a premium, coaching-focused strength facility can offer. Whether you're completely new to structured training, returning after time away, or an experienced athlete looking for a world-class training environment, this trial gives you the chance to experience the XGYM difference without any risk.

Beginners benefit from personalized coaching and a supportive community that prioritizes proper technique. Experienced lifters appreciate our advanced programming, state-of-the-art equipment, and access to performance testing. Athletes from all sports discover how strength training accelerates their performance on the field or court.

If you've been training alone or in a commercial gym, you'll notice the difference immediately. XGYM is built for people who take their training seriously and want to train in an environment that matches their intensity and commitment.
