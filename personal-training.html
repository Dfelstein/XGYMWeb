Our Approach

Evidence-Based Personal Training

At XGYM, your personal training program doesn't start with guesswork. It starts with comprehensive testing. We conduct DEXA scans, metabolic assessments, and strength evaluations to understand your body composition, metabolic capacity, and functional strengths and weaknesses.

From this data foundation, our coaches build a fully customised training program tailored specifically to your goals, current fitness level, and physiology. Your program evolves through structured periodisation blocks, with regular testing milestones to ensure you're progressing and adjusting as needed.

This isn't generic programming. It's your blueprint for success, informed by science and delivered by coaches who understand strength, performance, and body composition.

Who Is Personal Training For?

Beginners Starting Strong: If you're new to structured training, personal training accelerates your progress and ensures you build healthy movement patterns from day one. No guesswork, just expert guidance.

Experienced Athletes: Whether you're chasing strength records, muscle gain, or athletic performance, our coaches design periodised programs that challenge you appropriately and drive measurable progress.

Injury Recovery & Prevention: Coming back from injury? Our coaches understand rehabilitation principles and design progressions that build strength safely. We also teach injury prevention strategies for long-term health.

Competition Prep: Preparing for a powerlifting meet, bodybuilding competition, or fitness event? We specialise in peaking strategies and competition-specific programming.

Coach Credentials: All personal training at XGYM is delivered by qualified coaches with backgrounds in exercise science and strength sports. Your coach is invested in your success and brings real-world experience to every session.
