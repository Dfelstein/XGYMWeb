What Is Metabolic Testing

Your Resting Metabolic Rate (RMR)

Metabolic testing measures your Resting Metabolic Rate—the exact number of calories your body burns at rest just to maintain basic functions like breathing, circulation, and cell production. This number forms the foundation of effective nutrition planning.

Why does this matter? Most people estimate their calorie needs using generic formulas or online calculators. These estimates can be off by 300-500 calories per day, which completely sabotages fat loss and muscle gain goals. Your actual metabolism is unique based on your age, sex, genetics, muscle mass, hormonal status, and training history.

XGYM uses an indirect calorimetry metabolic analyzer that measures the oxygen you consume and carbon dioxide you produce. This shows exactly how many calories your body burns at rest—providing the precise starting point for your nutrition strategy. Combined with your activity level and training, we calculate your total daily energy expenditure (TDEE) and design macronutrient targets specifically for your goals.

What You'll Discover

Your Metabolic Profile

Your RMR

The exact number of calories your body burns at rest (typically 1200-2000+ depending on size, muscle mass, and metabolism). This is your caloric foundation for all nutrition planning.

Metabolic Efficiency

How efficiently your body uses fuel. Some people are "fat burners" while others are "carb utilizers." Understanding this optimizes your macronutrient ratios and energy availability.

Custom Targets

Based on your RMR, activity level, and goal (muscle gain, fat loss, or maintenance), we calculate your personalized daily calorie and macro targets for optimal results.

The Process

Your Metabolic Test Experience

01

12-Hour Fast

Fast overnight or for 12 hours before your test. No food, coffee, or caffeine. This ensures accurate baseline metabolic measurement.

02

Arrive Relaxed

Come to XGYM and relax for 10-15 minutes in a calm environment. We'll take your baseline measurements and position you comfortably for testing.

03

Breathe Into Device

Breathe normally through our metabolic analyzer for 15 minutes. It's non-invasive and requires no effort—just relax and breathe while we measure your oxygen consumption.

04

Get Your Report

Receive a detailed report showing your RMR, metabolic efficiency, respiratory quotient, and calculated TDEE based on your activity level.

05

Nutrition Blueprint

Your coach designs personalized calorie and macro targets for your specific goal. You'll receive a nutrition guide built on YOUR metabolism, not generic estimates.

06

Track & Adjust

Follow your personalized plan and retest after 8-12 weeks to see how your metabolism has adapted—especially important if you're in a sustained calorie deficit.
