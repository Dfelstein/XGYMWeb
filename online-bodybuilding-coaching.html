Online Coaching

What Is Online Coaching?

XGYM's online coaching program delivers fully customised bodybuilding and physique programming to athletes worldwide. Whether you're competing at the amateur or professional level, preparing for your first show, or building your physique for personal satisfaction, our coaches provide expert guidance across training, nutrition, posing, and competition strategy.

You maintain complete flexibility while receiving the programming precision and accountability of working with a dedicated elite coach. Adjustments are made based on your progress, feedback, and real-time updates, ensuring your program evolves with your physique and goals.

Your Program

What You Get With Online Coaching

Custom Training Program

Fully personalised training splits, rep ranges, exercise selection, and progression schemes designed specifically for your goals and current conditioning.

Weekly Check-ins

Direct communication with your coach every week. Discuss progress, ask questions, and receive adjustments in real-time based on how you're responding.

Nutrition & Macro Planning

Detailed macro targets for each phase of your prep—off-season building, cutting, and peak week. We adjust calories and macros based on your progress and metabolic response.

Posing Coaching

For competitors, we provide detailed posing guidance and critique. You'll learn mandatory poses, transitions, and how to present your physique optimally on stage.

Progress Photo Reviews

Submit regular progress photos for coaching feedback. We assess conditioning, proportion, and development to guide your training and nutrition adjustments.

Competition Prep Support

Complete competition preparation including peak week carb loading, water management, tanning preparation, and stage-day strategy for your show.

Our Coaching Philosophy

We believe in periodised training and nutrition that respects your body's need for progressive challenge and strategic recovery. Off-season phases focus on muscle building with controlled calorie surpluses and heavy strength work. Training becomes more volume-intensive as we transition into conditioning phases, with nutrition adjusted to preserve muscle while dropping body fat strategically.

Peak week preparation is approached with precision. We use evidence-based carbohydrate loading, controlled sodium depletion, and hydration strategies to maximise fullness and hardness on stage. This isn't generic cookie-cutter prep—it's customised to your body, your history, and your individual response patterns.

Long-term development is our priority. We're not chasing one show. We build sustainable systems that allow you to compete repeatedly, improve year after year, and maintain your health throughout the process.
