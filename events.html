Our Event History

XGYM has a proud history of hosting strength sports events and educational workshops in Castle Hill. Since opening, we've delivered powerlifting competitions, bodybuilding seminars, nutrition workshops, and community fitness challenges that have brought together athletes and fitness enthusiasts from across Sydney. Our events combine competition, education, and community—creating experiences that go beyond the gym floor.

Whether you're a competitor looking to test your strength, an athlete wanting to deepen your knowledge, or someone seeking to join our fitness community, our events offer something for everyone.
